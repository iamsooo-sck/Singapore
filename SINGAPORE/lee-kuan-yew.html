Personal Information

Name: Lee Kuan Yew

Date of Birth: September 16, 1923

Date of Death: March 23, 2015

Nationality: Singaporean

Occupation: Politician, Lawyer, Statesman

Achievements

  • Served as Singapore's first Prime Minister from 1959 to 1990.
  • Transformed Singapore from a resource-deficient British colony into one of the world's wealthiest nations.
  • Established Singapore's independence in 1965 and laid the foundation for its stability.
  • Implemented policies that fostered industrialization, education, and urban development.
  • Promoted meritocracy and created a pro-business environment that attracted global investments.
  • Initiated public housing programs that provided affordable homes to over 80% of Singaporeans.
