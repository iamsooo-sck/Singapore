About Hainanese Chicken Rice

Hainanese Chicken Rice is one of Singapore's most beloved dishes, known for its simplicity and rich flavors. The dish features poached or roasted chicken served with fragrant rice cooked in chicken broth, accompanied by tangy chili sauce, ginger paste, and a savory soy-based dressing. Often regarded as a comfort food, it showcases Singapore's cultural heritage and culinary expertise in making simple ingredients shine.

Recipe for Hainanese Chicken Rice

  1. Poach a whole chicken (about 1.5 kg) in boiling water until fully cooked (around 35-40 minutes). Save the broth for later.
  2. Cook rice by saut√©ing minced garlic and ginger in oil, then add washed rice and chicken broth. Simmer until the rice is fluffy.
  3. Prepare condiments: mix soy sauce with sesame oil for dressing, blend fresh chili and garlic for chili sauce, and prepare ginger paste with grated ginger and oil.
  4. Carve the chicken and serve it over rice, garnished with cucumber slices and spring onions.
  5. Serve with the prepared sauces and a bowl of chicken broth for a complete experience.

Hainanese Chicken Rice is the perfect balance of flavors, making it a must-try for anyone seeking to taste Singapore's culinary heritage.
