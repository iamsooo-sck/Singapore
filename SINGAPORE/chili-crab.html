About Chili Crab

Chili Crab is one of Singapore's most iconic dishes, celebrated for its perfect balance of sweetness and spiciness. The dish features fresh crabs stir-fried in a thick, tangy sauce made from chili, tomato, and egg. Often served with steamed or fried mantou buns to soak up the flavorful gravy, Chili Crab is a must-try for anyone visiting Singapore.

Recipe for Chili Crab

  1. Clean and prepare fresh crabs (approximately 1 kg).
  2. Mix sauce ingredients: 4 tbsp tomato paste, 2 tbsp chili paste, 1 tbsp soy sauce, and 1/2 cup chicken stock.
  3. Heat oil in a wok and saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  4. Add the sauce mixture to the wok and simmer until thickened.
  5. Add crabs to the wok and toss in the sauce until cooked (about 10 minutes).
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve with mantou buns.

Enjoy this authentic Singaporean dish with a side of steamed or fried mantou buns to soak up the rich gravy!
