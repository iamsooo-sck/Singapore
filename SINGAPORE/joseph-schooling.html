Personal Information

Name: Joseph Isaac Schooling

Date of Birth: June 16, 1995

Nationality: Singaporean

Occupation: Former Professional Swimmer

Achievements

  • Won Singapore's first-ever Olympic gold medal in the 100m butterfly at the 2016 Rio Olympics.
  • Set an Olympic record with a time of 50.39 seconds in the 100m butterfly event.
  • Multiple gold medalist at the Southeast Asian Games and Asian Games.
  • Represented Singapore at the 2012 and 2016 Olympics, inspiring a new generation of athletes.
  • Graduated from the University of Texas at Austin, where he was part of the Texas Longhorns swimming team.
