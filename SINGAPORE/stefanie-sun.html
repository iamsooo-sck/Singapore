Personal Information

Name: Stefanie Sun (Sng Ee Tze)

Date of Birth: July 23, 1978

Nationality: Singaporean

Occupation: Singer, Songwriter

Achievements

  • Debuted in 2000 with the album *Yan Zi*, which sold over 330,000 copies in Taiwan and 200,000 copies in China.
  • Won the Golden Melody Award for Best New Artist in 2000.
  • Released multiple best-selling albums, including *My Desired Happiness* (2000) and *Kite* (2001).
  • Sold over 30 million albums throughout her career, making her one of Singapore's best-selling artists.
  • Performed the Singapore National Day Parade theme songs "We Will Get There" (2002) and "One United People" (2003).
