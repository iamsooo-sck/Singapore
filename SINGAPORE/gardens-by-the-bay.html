About Gardens By The Bay

Gardens by the Bay in Singapore is a breathtaking urban oasis that combines nature, art, and technology. Spanning 101 hectares, the park features iconic attractions like the towering Supertrees, which light up spectacularly at night, and two futuristic conservatories: the Flower Dome, showcasing diverse global flora, and the Cloud Forest, home to a stunning indoor waterfall. Visitors can also stroll along the OCBC Skyway for panoramic views of Marina Bay or explore the whimsical Floral Fantasy.
