About Sentosa Island

Sentosa Island is Singapore's premier resort destination, offering a mix of leisure, adventure, and relaxation. Originally a fishing village and later a British military base, it was transformed into a recreational haven in 1972. The island spans 500 hectares and features lush greenery, golden beaches, luxury resorts, and thrilling attractions. Visitors can enjoy iconic spots like Universal Studios Singapore, S.E.A. Aquarium, Adventure Cove Waterpark, and Fort Siloso. Sentosa also boasts world-class golf courses, a deep-water marina, and vibrant nightlife. Easily accessible via cable car, boardwalk, or monorail, Sentosa is a must-visit for anyone looking to experience Singapore's blend of nature, history, and modern entertainment

Why Visit Sentosa Island?

Sentosa Island is a destination like no other, offering a perfect mix of adventure, relaxation, and luxury. It's home to top attractions like Universal Studios Singapore, where movie magic comes alive, and the S.E.A. Aquarium, where you can marvel at vibrant marine life. For thrill-seekers, Adventure Cove Waterpark and Skyline Luge Sentosa provide unforgettable experiences, while history enthusiasts can explore Fort Siloso's wartime relics. Sentosa pristine beaches, such as Siloso and Palawan, are ideal for unwinding, and the islands world-class resorts, dining, and nightlife ensure you'll enjoy every moment. Accessible via cable car or monorail, Sentosa delivers everything from family fun to romantic escapes, making it a must-visit when in Singapore.
